Manual Health Checks

No device? Here is how to estimate your vitals.

Checking Heart Rate (Pulse)

  1. Place two fingers (index and middle) on your wrist, below the thumb.
  2. Feel for a pulsing sensation.
  3. Count the beats for 15 seconds.
  4. Multiply by 4 to get beats per minute (BPM).
Normal Range: 60 - 100 BPM

High Blood Pressure Signs

BP cannot be measured accurately without a cuff, but watch for these symptoms:

  • Severe headaches
  • Nosebleed
  • Fatigue or confusion
  • Vision problems
  • Chest pain

High Blood Sugar Signs

Watch for these indicators of Hyperglycemia:

  • Increased thirst and dry mouth
  • Frequent urination
  • Blurred vision
  • Unintended weight loss
